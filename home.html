How does it work?


Instead of wasting food in restaurants, the Delivery System helps providing the less fortunate people with food by connecting restaurants and food charities. The link can be anyone, even you! Sign up now and make the difference with us.



Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to elimate food waste, helping out people in the community who are going through difficult times. No one should be worried about where their next meal is coming from, and if they can afford it.


VISION: We hope to one day eliminate food waste from all major food outlets, putting that food to good use, lowering the overall hunger and poverty issues faced by many around us.
